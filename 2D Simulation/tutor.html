Hello! Welcome to the Block World Tutorial!

General Instructions:
 In this game, you will see a table of two-sided blocks with different colors and letters on each side. You will be paired with a partner and given a task. One-way communication between the two of you is used so you can work together to complete the given task. Once the task has been completed, the user with access to the End button may click it to end the game.Try to complete the task as efficiently as possible.
